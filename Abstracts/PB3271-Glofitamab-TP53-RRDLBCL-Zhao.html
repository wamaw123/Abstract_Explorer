Population & Aim (N=20)

R/R DLBCL Pts with TP53 mutations. Aim: Efficacy & safety of chemo-free Glofitamab combos.

Intervention

Glofitamab + (BTKi / Sintilimab / Lenalidomide / Polatuzumab Vedotin). Real-world data.

Generally mild AEs (G1/2 CRS, tumor flare). High efficacy & good tolerability in this high-risk group.

PB3271 - Chemo-free regimens containing Glofitamab are significantly beneficial for relapsed/refractory diffuse large B cell lymphoma patients with TP53 gene mutations: Real-world data from multi-centers in China

Xia Zhao, Zhihe Liu, Junqing Xu, et al.

EHA 2025 Congress | Abstract #PB3271 | Publication Only

Background

  • TP53 gene mutations are an independent risk factor for the overall survival of patients with diffuse large B cell lymphoma (DLBCL), even with 1L R-CHOP or Pola-R-CHP.
  • Relapse/refractory episodes are common in these patients.
  • No standard regimen exists for R/R DLBCL patients with TP53 gene mutations.

Aims

To investigate the clinical efficacy and adverse events of chemo-free regimens containing glofitamab for patients with R/R DLBCL with TP53 gene mutations.

Methods & Study Design

  • Retrospective, multi-center study (3 hospitals in China).
  • Enrollment period: April 2024 - February 2025.
  • Patients: R/R DLBCL with TP53 gene mutations (exons 5-8 detected by NGS).
  • Intervention: Chemo-free regimens containing glofitamab combined with:
    • BTK inhibitors (acalabrutinib, zanubrutinib, or obutinib)
    • Sintilimab (PD-1 inhibitor)
    • Lenalidomide
    • Polatuzumab vedotin
  • Response assessment: PET-CT by local investigators.
