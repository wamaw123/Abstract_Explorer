Population & Aim (N=14)

Newly diagnosed elderly DLBCL NOS (>60 yrs). Aim: Evaluate R-CHOP-Len-Ibr effectiveness.

Intervention

R-CHOP/R-mini-CHOP + Lenalidomide (10mg D1-10) + Ibrutinib (420mg D1-10) x6 courses.

Key Efficacy (N=14)

CRR: 92.9% (13/14). 1 PD, 1 early relapse/death. 13 alive at median f/u 20 mo.

R-CHOP/R-mini-CHOP-Len-Ibr is an effective protocol for elderly DLBCL NOS. (Safety data not detailed).

PB3301 - The R-CHOP-Lenalidomide-Ibrutinib protocol in the treatment of elderly patients with diffuse B-cell large cell lymphoma, not otherwise specified.

Aminat Magomedova, Yana Mangasarova, Nelli Gabeeva, et al.

EHA 2025 Congress | Abstract #PB3301 | Publication Only

Background

  • R-CHOP is standard for DLBCL, but results are unsatisfactory, especially in high-risk and elderly patients (median age 60 yrs).
  • Different approaches are needed for elderly patients.
  • SMART-START study showed good results in elderly de novo DLBCL. This study used a modified, less costly version.

Aims

To evaluate the effectiveness of R-CHOP-Lenalidomide-Ibrutinib therapy in elderly patients (>60 years) with DLBCL, not otherwise specified (NOS).

Methods & Study Design

  • Inclusion: Newly diagnosed DLBCL NOS (WHO 2008/2017), >60 years, no prior chemo/radiation. N=14.
  • No radiation therapy or CNS prophylaxis.
  • Response assessment: Lugano 2014 criteria.
  • Treatment: R-CHOP + Lenalidomide (10mg D1-10) + Ibrutinib (420mg D1-10) for 6 courses.
  • Patients >80 years received R-mini-CHOP with the same Lenalidomide-Ibrutinib schedule.

Efficacy Results (N=14)

  • Complete Remission (CR) achieved in 13/14 (92.9%) patients.
  • Disease Progression (DP): 1 patient (7.1%) with TP53 mutation.
  • Early Relapse & Death from PD: 1 patient (7.1%).
  • Alive at median follow-up of 20 months (range 5-57): 13 patients.
  • Median CR follow-up: 17 months (range 3-54 months).

Safety Summary

Specific adverse event details and grading were not provided in the abstract text.

Summary & Conclusion

  • R-CHOP/R-mini-CHOP-Lenalidomide-Ibrutinib is an effective protocol for the treatment of elderly patients with DLBCL, NOS.
  • The research is ongoing.
