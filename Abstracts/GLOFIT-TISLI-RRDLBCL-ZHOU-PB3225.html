Visual Summary: PB3225 - Glofitamab + Tislelizumab in R/R DLBCL (Pilot Study)

Population & Aim (N=10)

R/R DLBCL Pts (≥18y, ≥2 prior LOT). Aim: Evaluate efficacy & safety of Glofitamab + Tislelizumab.

Intervention (Pilot Study)

C1: Obi D1, Glofit D8(2.5mg)/D15(10mg). C2+: Glofit 30mg D1, Tislelizumab 200mg D8. Up to 12 cycles.

Key Efficacy (N=9 evaluable, mF/U 6.3mo)

ORR: 66.7% (6/9). CR: 55.6% (5/9). OS & PFS rates at data cutoff: 50%.

Safety & Conclusion

Favorable safety. CRS: G1 50%, G2 10%. No G3/4 CRS/ICANS. G3/4 hematol. AEs 50%. Encouraging outcomes.

PB3225 - Efficacy and safety of Glofitamab combined with Tislelizumab in relapsed/refractory diffuse large B-cell lymphoma: A pilot study in China

De Zhou, Wenjuan Yu, Ehsan Ghoushi, et al.

EHA 2025 Congress | Abstract #PB3225 | Publication Only

Background

  • Prognosis for relapsed/refractory (R/R) diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL) remains poor.
  • Glofitamab (CD20×CD3 bispecific antibody) monotherapy showed a 39% CR rate in a Phase 2 study.
  • Efficacy may be limited by T-cell numbers/function; Immune Checkpoint Inhibitors (ICIs) like Tislelizumab (anti-PD-1) may restore T-cell activity.
  • ICIs combined with CAR T-cell therapy have shown prolonged DOR.

Aims

To evaluate the efficacy and safety of glofitamab combined with tislelizumab in patients with R/R DLBCL in a pilot study.

Methods & Study Design

  • Study Type: Pilot study (China). Registered Phase 2: ChiCTR2400091464.
  • Patient Population: DLBCL pts (≥18 years), R/R after ≥2 prior lines of therapy.
  • Treatment Regimen (21-day cycles):
    • Cycle 1: Obinutuzumab 1000mg D1, Glofitamab 2.5mg D8, Glofitamab 10mg D15.
    • Subsequent Cycles (if no G≥2 CRS in C1): Glofitamab 30mg D1, Tislelizumab 200mg D8.
  • Treatment Duration: Pts with SD/PR continued up to 12 cycles. Pts with CR after 6 cycles entered follow-up.
  • Primary Endpoint: Safety.
  • Secondary Endpoints: CR rate, ORR, DOR, PFS, OS.
  • Response Assessment: 2014 Lugano criteria. AE/CRS Grading: CTCAE v5.0 / ASTCT criteria.
  • Enrollment Period: Feb 20, 2024 - June 11, 2024.

Patient Characteristics (N=10)

  • Median Age: 68.5 years (range, 25-80).
  • Gender: 6 Female, 4 Male.
  • Median Prior Lines of Therapy: 3.5.
  • All patients refractory to last line of therapy.
  • Median Follow-up: 6.3 months (range, 2.4-11.5). No patients lost to follow-up.

Efficacy & Safety Results

Safety (N=10):

  • CRS: 1 patient Grade 2, 5 patients Grade 1.
  • No Grade 3-4 CRS or ICANS observed.
  • Grade 3-4 Hematological AEs: 5 patients.
  • One death due to COVID-19 during Cycle 2.

Efficacy (N=9 evaluable):

  • Overall Response Rate (ORR): 66.7% (6/9).
  • Complete Response (CR): 55.6% (5/9).
  • Progressive Disease (PD): 33.3% (3/9).
  • Disease Recurrence among responders: 1/6 patients.
  • OS and PFS rates at data cutoff (Jan 31, 2025): Both 50%.

Summary & Conclusion

  • The combination of glofitamab and tislelizumab demonstrated a favorable safety profile in this pilot study of 10 R/R DLBCL patients.
  • Encouraging clinical outcomes were observed, with an ORR of 66.7% and a CR rate of 55.6% in the 9 efficacy-evaluable patients.
  • A Phase 2, single-arm, prospective study (ChiCTR2400091464) has been registered to further evaluate the efficacy and safety of this combination.
