PF984 - Pola-R-CHP performed well in POLARIX trial-ineligible and low risk (IPI 0-1) patients with previously untreated DLBCL: A prospective observational study

Yuhong Ren, Luya Cheng, Jingli Zhuang, et al.

EHA 2025 Congress | Abstract #PF984 | Poster Presentation

Background

  • Pola-R-CHP showed increased 5-year PFS in untreated DLBCL with IPI 2-5 (POLARIX study).
  • Limited evidence for Pola-R-CHP in patients outside POLARIX criteria (e.g., IPI 0-1, specific comorbidities).
  • Need to elucidate efficacy/safety in all DLBCL patients in real-world settings.

Aims

To investigate the efficacy and safety of Pola-R-CHP in untreated adult DLBCL patients of low risk (IPI 0-1) or ineligible for POLARIX trial.

Methods & Study Design

  • Prospective observational cohort study (NCT06203652) at Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University.
  • Enrollment: April 1, 2023 - Oct 31, 2024. N=117 successive DLBCL patients.
  • Inclusion: ≥1 cycle Pola-R-CHP as 1L, >18 years, informed consent. Comorbidities not excluded.
  • Intervention: Polatuzumab 1.8 mg/kg, Rituximab 375 mg/m², Cyclophosphamide 750 mg/m², Doxorubicin 50 mg/m² (all IV D1); Prednisone 100 mg PO QD (D1-5). q21d x 6 cycles.
  • Assessment: PET/CT or CT every 3 cycles. If SD/PD after 3 cycles, 2L treatment.
  • Response: Lugano 2014. AEs: NCI-CTCAE 5.0.
  • Primary Endpoint: CR rate after 6 cycles of Pola-R-CHP.
  • Secondary Endpoints: Safety, 12-month PFS.

Efficacy Results (Median F/U: 7.1 months)

Primary Endpoint (CR rate after 6 cycles):

  • All patients (N=72 completed 6 cycles): 80.6%
  • Low risk (IPI 0-1) subgroup (N=12 from image): 91.7%
  • POLARIX trial-ineligible subgroup (N=34 from image): 73.5%

12-Month PFS Rate:

  • All patients (N=72 cohort): 90.5%
  • Low risk (IPI 0-1) subgroup: 100.0%
  • POLARIX trial-ineligible subgroup: 88.4%

Median PFS and OS not reached in all groups.

Data for CR/PR at 6 Cycles based on provided image (Figure 1 in abstract).

Safety Summary (N=117)

Most common Grade 3-5 Adverse Events:

  • Lung infection: 20.5%
  • Neutropenia: 17.9%
  • Leukopenia: 12.8%

Most patients recovered with best supportive care.

Peripheral neuropathy: 20 patients (19/20 were Grade 1-2).

Dose de-escalation/discontinuation of polatuzumab vedotin:

  • 4 patients in trial-ineligible group.
  • 1 patient in trial-eligible group.

Summary & Conclusion

  • Pola-R-CHP performed well in POLARIX trial-ineligible and low-risk (IPI 0-1) DLBCL patients as first-line therapy in this real-world prospective cohort.
  • Safety profiles were manageable, even in patients ineligible for POLARIX trial.
  • Ongoing treatment and follow-up will be further updated.
